Assorted Food Recipe

I don't believe in food blogs, but for this I'll make an exception.

Blog

When I was let go from my job, I had to come up with an affordable meal anyone can make for FREE.

Ingredients

  • Food waste from local restaurant's dumpsters
  • Waste oil from local restaurants
  • 1 staff member from local restaurant
  • Just a bit of magic

Instructions

  1. Heat up the waste oil on top of a burning dumpster, about medium high heat
  2. Dump in the food, order doesn't matter
  3. This is where the magic comes in. You need to cast a spell from the order of death while speaking Belphegor's name backwards 3 times
  4. Sacrifice the staff member to complete the spell

Note:Do not eat the sacrificed staff member or Belphegor lord of gluttony will consume your soul instead.
