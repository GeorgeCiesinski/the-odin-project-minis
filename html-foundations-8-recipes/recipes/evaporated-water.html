Evaporated Water Recipe

I don't believe in food blogs. I decided to honor the rule again.

Ingredients

  • Himalayan mountain water (from Mt. Everest if possible)

Instructions

  1. Purchase a Catch Me Who Can from your local steam locomotive shop
  2. Use a plasma torch to open the steam dome
  3. Load some clean coal and the Himalayan mountain water into the steam locomotive
  4. Enjoy some steaming hot evaporated water straight from the source
