Rice Recipe

I don't believe in food blogs, so without further adieu, here is the recipe.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Himalayan mountain water (from Mt. Everest if possible)
  • Himalayan salt

Instructions

  1. Cook the rice in a large saucepan over medium-high heat until it is cooked.
  2. Add the Himalayan salt and Himalayan mountain water and bring to a boil.
  3. Reduce the heat and simmer for 20 minutes.
  4. Serve the rice with a side of rice ketchup.
